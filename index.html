Creative Passion

I love being creative and producing awesome things. From pretty pictures to meaningful products, I enjoy a wide variety of projects and have a diverse portfolio to show for it. Aside from being a creative person, I also enjoy teaching others how to draw and design through written tutorials and guided lessons.


More Info

Design Process

I always begin with research to learn about what my end goals are. Next, I sketch out my ideas to get a basic visual foundation. Once I have my composition down, I'll move my designs to their respective mediums and begin refining them until it's final. Of course, feedback is absolutely key to make sure my designs are on point.

Honors

My design direction for "Zombie Critters" won the Best Art Award (Summer 2014) during demo day at the Computer History Musuem in San Jose. My thesis project was also featured on the Academy of Art University Graduate Showcase.
